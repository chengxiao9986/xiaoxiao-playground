## Learning Notes

### New Words

| Word | Pronunciation | Meaning | Context |
|------|--------------|---------|---------|
| campaign | /kæmˈpeɪn/ | 活动/推广 | World Cup Campaign |
| upsell | /ˈʌpsel/ | 追加销售/升级推荐 | rewards upsell banner |
| mechanic | /məˈkænɪk/ | 机制/玩法 | same mechanic as "Install & Earn" |
| reuse | /riːˈjuːz/ | 复用 | Bing app reuses Mobile Web SERP |
| metrics | /ˈmetrɪks/ | 指标/数据 | new install metrics |
| referral | /rɪˈfɜːrəl/ | 推荐/转介 | referral offer promotion |
| kick off | /kɪk ɒf/ | 启动 | kick off the referral offer promotion |
| targeting | /ˈtɑːrɡɪtɪŋ/ | 目标定于 | we're targeting March 20th |
| drop | /drɒp/ | 下降 | that number dropped to around 600 |
| bring back up | — | 拉回来/恢复 | bring those numbers back up |

### Key Takeaways

- **in terms of** 从…方面来看 — 引出数据话题的万能开头
- **were seeing** 过去进行时描述一段时间内的持续数据
- **there's been no...** 现在完成时表示"一直没有"
- **expect [sth] to [do]** 预期某事将会…
- **kick off** / **bring back up** — 两个会议高频 phrasal verb
