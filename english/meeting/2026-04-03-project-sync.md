### Key Expressions

**1.** "We're promoting [X] through [channels] to drive [goal]"
- 我们通过...渠道推广...以实现...目标
- **Purpose structure**: "to drive" 表达推广的目的
- Example: "We're promoting the referral offer through multiple channels to drive broader reach."

**2.** "On the [topic] front, [update]"
- 在...方面，[进展更新]
- **Topic transition**: professional way to switch between different aspects
- Example: "On the timeline front, the homepage banner is set to launch on March 20th."

**3.** "We're planning to run [something] from [date] through [date]"
- 我们计划从...到...运行/执行...
- **Time range expression**: "from X through Y" = 从X到Y（包含结束日期）
- Example: "We're planning to run those from March 30th through June 30th."

**4.** "One blocker we're working through — [issue]"
- 我们正在解决的一个障碍是...
- **Problem reporting**: "working through" 强调正在积极解决
- Example: "One blocker we're working through — there's a Max adaptation issue."

**5.** "[Person] is looped in on [issue]"
- [某人]已经参与/知情此事
- **Collaboration expression**: "loop in" = 让某人参与或了解情况
- Example: "Jean Wood is looped in on that, and we're coordinating to get that resolved."

**6.** "This is a smaller effort — we're making minimal [changes] to [goal]"
- 这是一个较小的工作 — 我们只做最小的...来...
- **Scope framing**: 说明项目规模和范围
- Example: "This is a smaller effort — we're making minimal app-side changes to align with service updates."

**7.** "To make sure [X] stays consistent across [surfaces/platforms]"
- 确保...在各个...保持一致
- **Consistency goal**: 常用于跨平台开发
- Example: "To make sure the experience stays consistent across all surfaces."

### Key Takeaways

- **drive broader reach** 扩大覆盖面 — 推广活动的常见目标
- **loop in** 让某人参与 — 团队协作常用表达
- **blocker** 阻碍项 — 项目管理术语
- **from X through Y** 从X到Y — 注意 "through" 包含结束日期
- **parity** 对等/一致性 — 技术对齐的专业说法
- **surfaces** 平台/界面 — 指不同的用户接触点（web, app, etc.）
- **on the X front** 在X方面 — 优雅的话题转换表达
