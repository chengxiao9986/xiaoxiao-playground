# Expression Patterns

## #meeting | 会议场景

### We wrapped up [something]
完成某项工作（口语化表达，适合团队会议）

- "We wrapped up the foundational architecture this week."
- Grammar: "wrap up" 是动词短语，比 "complete" 或 "finish" 更口语化和自然
- Source: 2026-04-03-weekly-sync

### Looking ahead to [time period], we're planning to [action]
展望未来某个时间段，我们计划做某事

- "Looking ahead to next week, we're planning to kick off user testing."
- Grammar: "looking ahead" 是现在分词短语作状语，表示将视角转向未来
- Source: 2026-04-03-weekly-sync

### On the [topic] front, we're seeing [results]
在某个方面，我们看到某些结果

- "On the KPI front, we're seeing some really promising results."
- Grammar: "on the X front" 是固定搭配，用于话题转换，front 在这里表示"方面，领域"
- Source: 2026-04-03-weekly-sync

### [Metric] has jumped from [X] to [Y]
某指标从X跃升到Y

- "Our response accuracy has jumped from 65% to 82%."
- Grammar: 现在完成时 + "jump from X to Y"，强调显著的提升变化
- Source: 2026-04-03-weekly-sync

### We've managed to [achieve something] by [amount/percentage]
我们成功做到了某事（暗示经过努力）

- "We've managed to reduce the average response time by 40%."
- Grammar: "manage to" 表示克服困难后成功做到，比简单的 "we reduced" 更能体现团队努力
- Source: 2026-04-03-weekly-sync

### Right now, we're focused on [current work]
目前我们专注于某项工作

- "Right now, we're focused on prompt engineering optimization."
- Grammar: "right now" 时间状语 + 现在进行时，表示当前正在进行的重点工作
- Source: 2026-04-03-weekly-sync
