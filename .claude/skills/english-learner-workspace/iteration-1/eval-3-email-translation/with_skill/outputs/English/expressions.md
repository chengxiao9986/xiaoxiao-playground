## #reading | 阅读场景

### By default, [something] is/are [done].
描述默认行为

- "By default Notion formulas are converted to Obsidian syntax."
- Grammar: 被动语态描述系统行为
- Source: 2026-04-02

### [Action] so that [purpose].
说明目的

- "Adds a notion-id property to pages so that future imports can skip pages that have already been imported."
- Grammar: so that 目的从句 + have already been done 现在完成时被动
- Source: 2026-04-02

### [Option 1]. Alternatively, you can [Option 2].
提供替代方案

- "Static values will be saved. Alternatively, you can import all formulas as static values."
- Source: 2026-04-02

### If [condition], [result] will be [done] instead.
条件 + 替代方案

- "If any Notion syntax is not supported the static values will be saved instead."
- Grammar: 第一条件句 + 被动语态 + instead 收尾
- Source: 2026-04-02

### [Imperative] if you don't have [issue].
条件指引

- "Leave as 'cover' if you don't have conflicts with existing properties."
- Grammar: 祈使句 + if 条件从句
- Source: 2026-04-02

### [Something] will be [done] according to [basis].
描述依据

- "Attachments will be saved according to your vault's attachment folder settings."
- Grammar: according to = 根据
- Source: 2026-04-02

## #meeting | 会议场景

### Quick update on [topic].
会议开场

- "Quick update on the World Cup Campaign."
- Source: 2026-04-02-shiproom

### We're still in [stage] stage.
汇报当前状态

- "We're still in early planning stage."
- Grammar: in + [adj] + stage 表示处于某阶段
- Source: 2026-04-02-shiproom

### In terms of [metric] — [context], we were seeing [number].
引出数据

- "In terms of new installs — when we had the email promotion running, we were seeing about twenty-five hundred per day."
- Grammar: in terms of 引出话题 + were seeing 过去进行时描述持续数据
- Source: 2026-04-02-shiproom

### After [event], that number dropped to [number].
描述数据下降

- "After the promotion ended and there's been no marketing action, that number dropped to around six hundred per day."
- Grammar: there's been no 现在完成时表持续状态 + dropped to 降至
- Source: 2026-04-02-shiproom

### Next step — [action] to [goal].
说明下一步

- "So the next step here is to kick off the Referral Offer promotion to bring those numbers back up."
- Grammar: kick off 启动 + bring back up 恢复 — 两个实用 phrasal verb
- Source: 2026-04-02-shiproom

### We expect [something] to [result].
预期效果

- "We expect those to help push the numbers back up."
- Grammar: expect [sth] to [do] 预期某事将会…
- Source: 2026-04-02-shiproom

### That's all from my side. Any questions?

## #email | 邮件沟通

### Due to [recent change], we've discovered that [problem].
报告问题发现

- "Due to recent API changes, we've discovered that several integration test cases are now failing."
- Grammar: Due to 引出原因 + we've discovered that 比 we found 更正式 + are now failing 现在进行时强调当前状态
- Source: 2026-04-02

### We have identified the root cause: [explanation].
说明根本原因

- "We have identified the root cause: the response schema has changed, and our current parsing logic isn't handling the newly added fields."
- Grammar: 冒号(:) 引出具体解释 + has changed 现在完成时表示最近变化及当前影响 + isn't handling 表示持续问题
- Source: 2026-04-02

### We will [action] by [deadline] and [follow-up action] to ensure [goal].
承诺修复时间

- "We will fix this issue by Friday and re-run all regression tests to ensure there are no other problems."
- Grammar: by Friday 截止日期表达 + re-run 前缀re-表示再次 + to ensure 表达目的
- Source: 2026-04-02

### [Component] doesn't handle [new feature/change].
描述技术问题

- "Our parsing logic doesn't handle the newly added fields."
- Grammar: doesn't handle = 不处理/未处理 + newly added 新增的
- Source: 2026-04-02
